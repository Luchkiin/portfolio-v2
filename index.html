About

Hi, I'm Lucas, a Stockholm-based developer and designer with a genuine interest for front-end development and UX/UI design.

I originally come from a more technical background but consider myself today as a versatile designer and developer who uses different approaches in solving various problems that comes my way. My expertise is based on a three-year bachelor's degree in systems science but also stems from personal initiatives regarding different skills I have picked up along my learning career path in the field of IT and design.

Things that I am good at are front-end development and UX/UI design, where I have an eye for detail in what I create. I have in addition to this also a mind for design and usability where my goal as a developer and designer is to create user-friendly and memorable products for the end-user to experience and use.

When I'm not working on creating things, I like to listen to my vinyl collection, workout and spend quality time with family & friends. Please feel free to let me know if you have any questions.
