About

Stockholm-based developer and designer with a genuine interest in UX/UI design and front-end development.

My expertise is based on a bachelor's degree in systems science but also stems from different skills I have picked up in the field of design and IT. These skills include developing techniques and design principles that interest and defines me as a designer.
