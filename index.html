About

Hi, I'm Lucas, a Stockholm-based designer and with a genuine interest for front-end development and UX/UI design.

My expertise is based on a bachelor's degree in systems science but also stems from different skills I have picked up in the field of design and IT. These skills include different developing techniques and design principles that interest and defines me as a designer and developer.

Things I excel at are UX/UI design, where I have an eye for detail in what I create. With my zealous way of working, I possess a mindset for design and usability where my end-goal is to create user-friendly and memorable products for the end-user to experience.

When I'm not working on creating things, I like to listen to my vinyl collection, workout and spend quality time with family & friends. Please feel free to let me know if you have any questions.
